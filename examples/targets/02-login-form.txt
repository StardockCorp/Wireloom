Notes:
- `window "Sign in"` renders the title in a centered title bar at the top.
- `header:` renders as a separated band below the title bar.
- `panel:` renders as a dashed-border content area with its children stacked vertically.
- `input placeholder="Email"` shows the placeholder text inside a bordered field.
- `button "Sign in" primary` renders with emphasized stroke (depicted here with square brackets).
- `footer:` renders as a separated band at the bottom.
