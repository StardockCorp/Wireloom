Target rendering for examples/12-tabs.wireloom
================================================

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                      Colonial Charter                      │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Government  │ Leaders  │ Ministers  │ Diplomats(3) │ …   │
│  ══════════   (underlined active tab)                      │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐ │
│ │ Currently viewing Government settings.                 │ │
│ │ Switch tabs to see other sections.                     │ │
│ └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘ │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Notes:
- `tabs:` renders as a horizontal bar of `tab` labels separated by subtle vertical lines.
- The `active` tab gets an underline or brightened stroke.
- `tab "Diplomats" badge="3"` shows a small pill `(3)` next to the label.
- Content below the tab bar is the "active tab's body" — in v0.2 there is no real
  tab-switching, whatever siblings follow the tabs container render as the body.
