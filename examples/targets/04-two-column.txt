Notes:
- `row:` renders its children side-by-side, filling the available width.
- `col 240:` is a fixed-width column of 240 pixels (depicted here as roughly the left 40% of content width).
- `col:` with no width takes the remaining space (fills).
- Both columns stack their children vertically.
- A vertical divider separates the columns.
