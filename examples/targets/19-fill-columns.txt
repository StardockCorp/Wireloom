Notes:
- Left `col 240:` and right `col 280:` take their exact pixel widths.
- Middle `col:` (bare, no width) defaults to `fill` in v0.2.
- Fill distribution: `(row_width − 240 − 280 − 2 gaps)` flows to the middle.
- Each column stacks its children vertically.
- `list` of `item` children renders as bulleted lines.
- `kv` rows in the right column lay value flush right against the col's right edge.

v0.1 compat note:
- In v0.1, a bare `col:` hugged content (intrinsic sizing). v0.2 changes the default to fill.
- If the old v0.1 behavior is wanted, specify widths explicitly for every column.
